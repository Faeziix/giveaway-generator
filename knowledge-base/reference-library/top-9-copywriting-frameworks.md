## Target Pain Point
Ad copywriters and creative strategists lack repeatable structure when writing ad copy. Every new ad feels like starting from scratch, leading to inconsistency and slow output. These 9 frameworks give them proven formulas they can apply to any product, any audience, and any ad format — turning copywriting from an art into a repeatable process.

## Content Structure
- 9 distinct copywriting frameworks (likely includes classics like PAS, AIDA, BAB, and proprietary/modified frameworks)
- Each framework includes: name, structure breakdown, step-by-step instructions, and example application
- Use-case guidance for when to deploy each framework (e.g., cold traffic vs. retargeting, short-form vs. long-form)
- Possibly includes fill-in-the-blank templates for each framework
- Bundled as a "Master Copywriting Bundle" suggesting comprehensive coverage

## What Makes It Effective
- **"Our Top 9" uses possessive framing** — the word "Our" signals insider access to Linear's own internal playbook, making users feel like they're getting proprietary knowledge rather than generic advice.
- **Frameworks are infinitely reusable** — one download, unlimited applications. The user gets a permanent toolkit, not a one-time resource, which dramatically increases perceived value.
- **Curated number (9) feels deliberate** — not 10 (rounded and generic), not 100 (overwhelming). Nine signals careful curation: these are the best ones, distilled.
- **"Master Copywriting Bundle" in the page title elevates positioning** — framing it as a "bundle" implies multiple valuable components packaged together, reinforcing the value stack.

## Industry Adaptations
- **B2B SaaS:** "Our 9 Sales Email Frameworks" — replace ad copywriting frameworks with outbound email structures (cold outreach, follow-up, re-engagement, case study pitch), keep the "Our" possessive framing
- **Creator/Coach:** "9 Story Frameworks for Selling on Social" — replace ad copy structures with social storytelling formats (transformation story, behind-the-scenes, myth-busting, vulnerability post), keep curated number
- **Real Estate:** "9 Listing Description Frameworks That Sell Homes Faster" — replace ad formats with property description structures (luxury, starter home, investment, fixer-upper), same fill-in-the-blank template approach
- **Professional Services:** "Our Top 9 Proposal Frameworks" — replace copywriting with proposal structures (problem-solution, ROI-focused, case-study-led, competitive displacement), keep the "Master Bundle" positioning
