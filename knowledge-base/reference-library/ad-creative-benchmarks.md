## Target Pain Point
Brands spending on paid media often have no idea what "good" looks like. They see their own CTR, CPA, and ROAS numbers in isolation but lack external reference points. This benchmark report gives them Linear's proprietary performance data so they can evaluate whether their creative is underperforming, on par, or best-in-class.

## Content Structure
- Benchmark metrics organized by ad format (static, video, UGC, etc.)
- Key KPIs covered: CTR, hook rate, hold rate, CPA, ROAS
- Performance tiers (below average, average, above average) for each metric
- Breakdowns by spend level or vertical where applicable
- Notes on methodology and data sources

## What Makes It Effective
- **Proprietary data is the ultimate trust signal** — benchmarks sourced from Linear's own campaigns can't be Googled, making this genuinely unique.
- **"Our" framing implies insider access** — positions the reader as getting privileged, behind-the-curtain information from a working agency.
- **Benchmarks have a long shelf life** — users bookmark and revisit this repeatedly as they launch new campaigns.
- **Low effort, high value exchange** — the reader gives an email and gets data that would normally require expensive tools or agency relationships.

## Taxonomy Mapping
Falls under **Validate** because it helps users assess and judge their own performance against external standards. It doesn't teach them how to create ads (Educate) or give them ready-made assets (Accelerate) — it provides the measuring stick. Could touch Educate if it includes interpretation guidance, but the core function is benchmarking.
