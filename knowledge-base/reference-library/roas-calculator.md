## Target Pain Point
Brands pour money into ads but can't pinpoint where their spend is leaking. They know ROAS isn't where it should be but lack a structured way to diagnose why. This calculator computes ROAS across channels and surfaces the specific bottlenecks — whether it's CPM, CTR, CVR, or AOV — dragging performance down.

## Content Structure
- Input fields for ad spend, revenue, impressions, clicks, and conversions by channel
- Auto-calculated ROAS, CPA, CPM, CTR, and CVR metrics
- Bottleneck diagnostic section that flags which metric is underperforming relative to benchmarks
- Benchmark comparison column with industry averages for DTC
- Summary view highlighting the highest-leverage fix

## What Makes It Effective
- **Solves a specific, urgent problem** — "where is my ad spend leaking?" is a question brand owners ask constantly. The specificity of the pain point drives high opt-in intent.
- **"& Bottleneck Finder" adds bonus value** — the name signals this is more than a basic calculator; there's a diagnostic layer, which increases perceived sophistication.
- **Interactive tool format** — users input their own numbers, so the output is uniquely theirs. This creates ownership and stickiness.
- **Benchmarks provide context** — raw numbers mean nothing without comparison; including industry benchmarks makes the tool immediately interpretable.

## Industry Adaptations
- **B2B SaaS:** "CAC Payback Calculator & Pipeline Bottleneck Finder" — replace ROAS with CAC:LTV, ad metrics with funnel stage conversion rates (MQL→SQL→Opp→Close), benchmarks against SaaS industry averages
- **Real Estate:** "Investment Property ROI Calculator & Cash Flow Analyzer" — replace ad spend with purchase price/rehab costs, ROAS with cap rate and cash-on-cash return, bottleneck finder becomes "deal killer" diagnostic
- **Health & Fitness:** "Client Acquisition Cost Calculator & Retention Analyzer" — replace ad channels with acquisition channels (social, referral, walk-in), add client lifetime value and churn rate metrics
- **Agency:** "Project Profitability Calculator & Scope Creep Detector" — replace ROAS with project margin, ad spend with billable hours vs. actual hours, bottleneck finder flags which project types leak profit

## Taxonomy Mapping
Falls under **Enable** because the core function is a diagnostic tool — it helps users analyze their own data and identify what to fix. It's not providing creative assets (Accelerate) or deep teaching (Educate); it's giving brands the infrastructure to make smarter ad spend decisions. Could touch Educate if the benchmarks section includes explanatory context, but the primary value is the calculator.
