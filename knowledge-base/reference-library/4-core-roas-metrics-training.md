## Target Pain Point
Brand owners and media buyers track ROAS as a single number without understanding the underlying metrics that drive it. They lack the knowledge to diagnose performance issues or communicate effectively with their teams about what's actually happening in their ad accounts. This training provides deep education on the 4 core metrics that compose ROAS.

## Content Structure
- Introduction framing why surface-level ROAS tracking is insufficient
- Deep dive into each of the 4 core metrics (likely CPM, CTR, CVR, AOV) with definitions, benchmarks, and interplay
- Real examples showing how each metric impacts overall ROAS
- Framework for diagnosing which metric to prioritize improving
- Actionable takeaways and next steps
- Estimated length: 15-30 minutes (long-form educational content)

## What Makes It Effective
- **Long-form video builds authority** — extended teaching positions Linear as a knowledgeable, trustworthy source, not just a content mill.
- **"In-Depth" signals comprehensive coverage** — the naming sets expectations that this isn't a surface-level overview, attracting serious operators.
- **YouTube as delivery drives subscriber growth** — the giveaway doubles as a channel-building asset; viewers who get value are likely to subscribe, creating an owned audience on a second platform.
- **Foundational topic with broad appeal** — every DTC brand running ads needs to understand ROAS metrics, making this a wide-funnel lead magnet.
