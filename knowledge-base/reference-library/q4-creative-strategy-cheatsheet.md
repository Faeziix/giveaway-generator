## Target Pain Point
Q4 is the highest-stakes period for DTC brands — Black Friday, Cyber Monday, holiday gifting. CPMs spike, competition intensifies, and the creative that worked in Q2 often falls flat. Brands need a strategy tailored to the unique dynamics of Q4, not recycled year-round advice. This cheat sheet gives them a seasonal creative playbook.

## Content Structure
- Q4 timeline and key dates (BFCM, holiday shipping deadlines, last-minute gifting windows)
- Creative strategy shifts for each Q4 phase (pre-sale hype, launch, sustain, last call)
- Ad angle recommendations: urgency, gifting, social proof, scarcity
- Format recommendations by phase (static vs. video vs. UGC emphasis)
- Copy frameworks and hook ideas tuned for holiday messaging
- Budget pacing considerations and how creative strategy ties to spend

## What Makes It Effective
- **Time-sensitive topic creates natural urgency** — brands searching for Q4 strategy are usually weeks away from needing it, making conversion intent high.
- **Seasonal specificity makes it feel tailored** — a "Q4 Creative Strategy" feels far more relevant than a generic "Ad Strategy Guide." The narrower scope increases perceived value.
- **Cheat sheet format suits the audience's time constraints** — Q4 is the busiest period; nobody has time for a 30-page ebook. Scannable format matches the reader's reality.
- **Repeatable annual relevance** — this resource resurfaces in value every year, giving it a longer effective lifespan than most lead magnets.
