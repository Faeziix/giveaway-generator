## Target Pain Point
Brands want to leverage AI for creative strategy — analyzing competitors, generating briefs, ideating concepts — but don't know how to set it up effectively. Most attempts produce generic, unusable AI output. This step-by-step tutorial walks them through building a custom AI creative strategist that actually produces useful, brand-specific strategic output.

## Content Structure
- Introduction explaining what an "AI creative strategist" can do and why it matters
- Prerequisites: tools needed (ChatGPT, Claude, or similar)
- Step-by-step setup instructions for configuring the AI with brand context, tone, and objectives
- Prompt templates for core creative strategy tasks (competitor analysis, brief generation, hook ideation, ad review)
- Tips for refining outputs and iterating on prompts
- Example outputs showing what good AI-generated creative strategy looks like

## What Makes It Effective
- **Leverages a trending topic (AI)** — anything AI-related has elevated curiosity and urgency right now, driving higher opt-in rates regardless of the specific audience.
- **"How to Build Your Own" implies a tangible, practical output** — the user expects to walk away with something they built, not just knowledge they absorbed. This makes the tutorial feel concrete and outcome-oriented.
- **Tutorial format with actionable steps** — step-by-step structure reduces perceived difficulty and increases completion rate; users feel guided rather than overwhelmed.
- **Positions Linear as forward-thinking** — offering AI-focused content signals that Linear operates at the cutting edge, which elevates brand perception among sophisticated buyers.
