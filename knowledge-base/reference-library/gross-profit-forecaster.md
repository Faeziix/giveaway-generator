## Target Pain Point
DTC brand owners and operators struggle with financial planning and margin visibility. They often lack a clear, dynamic way to model how changes in COGS, shipping, or pricing affect their bottom line. This spreadsheet lets them forecast gross profit by plugging in their own numbers, turning abstract financials into concrete projections.

## Content Structure
- Input fields for revenue, COGS, shipping costs, and other variable expenses
- Auto-calculated gross profit margins at different revenue scenarios
- Breakdown by product line or SKU (editable rows)
- Summary dashboard with key margin percentages
- Instructions tab explaining how to use and customize the sheet

## What Makes It Effective
- **Interactive fill-in-the-blank format** — users input their own data, making the output personally relevant and immediately actionable.
- **High perceived value** — financial modeling tools feel expensive and professional; getting one free feels like a steal.
- **Spreadsheet delivery is frictionless** — Google Sheets requires no software install, and users can duplicate and keep it forever.
- **Ongoing utility** — this isn't a one-time read; brands return to it monthly, keeping Linear top of mind.

## Taxonomy Mapping
Falls under **Enable** because it gives users a tool to do something themselves — in this case, financial forecasting. It's not teaching theory (Educate) or providing creative shortcuts (Accelerate); it's providing infrastructure for decision-making. Could arguably touch Educate if it includes instructional context around margin benchmarks, but the primary function is the calculator itself.
