# Quick Match: From 3 Inputs to Format Recommendations

Use this to skip the multi-step taxonomy navigation. Answer three questions, get 2-3 format recommendations.

---

## Input 1: What Does the Audience Need Most?

| They need to... | Category | Think of it as... |
|-----------------|----------|-------------------|
| **A.** LEARN something (they lack knowledge) | Educate | "Teach me" |
| **B.** DO something faster (they lack tools) | Enable | "Do it for me" |
| **C.** START something (they lack a starting point) | Accelerate | "Give me a head start" |
| **D.** UNDERSTAND their situation (they lack clarity) | Diagnose | "Show me where I stand" |
| **E.** ACCESS people or exclusivity | Connect | "Let me in" |
| **F.** PROVE or justify a decision | Validate | "Give me the evidence" |

---

## Input 2: What Can the Client Actually Build?

| Resource Level | Available Formats | Effort Range |
|---------------|-------------------|--------------|
| **Low** (writing only, no design/dev, <1 week) | Checklist, Cheat Sheet, Template Collection, Swipe File | 2-8 hours |
| **Medium** (design skills, spreadsheets, 1-2 weeks) | Calculator, Quiz/Assessment, SOP, Framework Collection | 8-40 hours |
| **High** (dev team, video, 1+ month) | Custom GPT/Tool, Benchmark Report, Guide/Ebook, Video Training | 40-100+ hours |

---

## Input 3: Buyer Stage

| Stage | Best Categories | The audience is... |
|-------|----------------|-------------------|
| **Awareness** (don't know you) | Educate, Diagnose | Discovering the problem or just learning about it |
| **Consideration** (comparing options) | Validate, Enable | Evaluating solutions, doing research |
| **Decision** (ready to act) | Accelerate, Enable | Ready to implement, need tools to start |

## Combining Inputs: Common Combos

These are the most common client scenarios and their best-fit formats:

| Scenario | Inputs | Top Recommendation |
|----------|--------|-------------------|
| "We need more top-of-funnel leads, fast" | Educate + Low + Awareness | **Cheat Sheet** on their most-asked question — distribute via **TOF Viral Post** (`playbooks/tof-viral-post.md`) |
